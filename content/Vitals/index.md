# Hex Survival Vitals

Survival in Hex Survival depends on managing four primary status bars. Failing to maintain these will lead to penalties, panic, or death.

## Vitals Directory

### [[Vitals/hunger|Hunger]]
Replenished by food. Drains significantly at [[Mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Midnight]].

### [[Vitals/thirst|Thirst]]
Replenished by water. Drains significantly at [[Mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Midnight]].

### [[Vitals/fear|Fear]]
Psychological strain from the darkness. Accumulates in unlit tiles.

### [[Vitals/action_points|Action Points (AP)]]
The currency for performing tasks and moving across the map.

---

## The [[Mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Midnight Toll]]
Every day at Midnight, your **Hunger** and **Thirst** bars are reduced by **40%**. This is the primary survival check of the game.
