# Ideas: [[ideas/new_constructions|New Constructions]]

Proposed additions to the [[base/constructions|Base Constructions]] list to improve town-wide utility.

## 1. Advanced Infrastructure
- **Reinforced Power Relay**:
    - **Requirements**: 2 [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]], 1 [[items/alloy_plate|Alloy Plate]], 1 [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]].
    - **Effect**: A [[items/power_pole|Power Pole]] with double HP that can survive one extra hit during the [[mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Nightly Siege]].
- **High-Intensity Searchlight**:
    - **Requirements**: 3 [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]], 2 [[items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]], 1 [[items/cracked_lens|Cracked Lens]].
    - **Effect**: Illuminates a 1-hex radius around the Base. Increases passive Power Drain by 5.

## 2. Utility Facilities
- **Recycling Station**:
    - **Requirements**: 5 [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]], 2 [[items/hydraulic_piston|Hydraulic Pistons]].
    - **Effect**: Increases the primary yield of all [[skills/salvage|Salvaging]] actions performed at the Base by +5%.
- **Decontamination Chamber**:
    - **Requirements**: 4 [[items/stone|Hardened Stone]], 2 [[items/chemical_sludge|Chemical Sludge]], 1 [[items/logic_core|Logic Core]].
    - **Effect**: Dramatically increases [[vitals/fear|Fear]] recovery rate while Resting at the Base.
