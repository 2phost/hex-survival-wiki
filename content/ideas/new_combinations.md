# Ideas: [[ideas/new_combinations|New Combinations]]

Proposed crafting recipes to utilize mid-tier and rare materials.

## 1. Equipment Recipes
- **Advanced Lantern**:
    - **Inputs**: 1 [[items/lamp_functioning|Functioning Lamp]] + 1 [[items/solar_cell|Solar Cell]].
    - **Output**: **Solar Lantern** (Provides light for 10 days; recharges 1 day for every Midnight spent on a lit hex).
- **Hardened Toolkit**:
    - **Inputs**: 1 [[items/rusty_tool|Rusty Tool]] + 1 [[items/alloy_plate|Alloy Plate]].
    - **Output**: **Industrial Wrench** (Grants +5% bonus to [[skills/salvage|Salvage]] yields).

## 2. Material Refinement
- **Stabilized Biofuel**:
    - **Inputs**: 2 [[items/biofuel_cell|Biofuel Cells]] + 1 [[items/chemical_sludge|Chemical Sludge]].
    - **Output**: **High-Density Biofuel** (+50% Power Refill; requires [[skills/generator_operations|Gen. Ops]] Lvl 3).
- **Optic Repair Kit**:
    - **Inputs**: 1 [[items/cracked_lens|Cracked Lens]] + 1 [[items/old_glass_bottle|Old Glass Bottle]] + 1 [[items/circuit_boards|Circuit Board]].
    - **Output**: **Restored Binoculars** (Increases visual range to see biomes of adjacent hexes).
