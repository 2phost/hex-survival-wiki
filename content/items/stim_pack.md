# Stim Pack

![[assets/items/stim_pack.png|150]]


### Core Properties
- **Rarity**: common
- **Category**: Consumable
- **Description**: Improvised stimulant that restores 2 AP. Any AP over your max is lost. Increases hunger and thirst slightly.

## Usage
- Consume to add 6 hunger penalty and add 6 thirst penalty and restore 2 AP.

## Consumable Stats
- **Hunger**: 6
- **Thirst**: 6
- **AP**: 2
