# Stim Pack
**Rarity**: common
**Category**: Consumable
**Description**: Improvised stimulant that restores 2 AP. Any AP over your max is lost. Increases hunger and thirst slightly.

## Usage
- Usage: Consume to add 6 hunger penalty and add 6 thirst penalty and restore 2 AP.

## Consumable Stats
- **Hunger**: 6
- **Thirst**: 6
- **AP**: 2

## Skill Requirements
- [[skills/stim_conditioning|Stim Conditioning]] Level 1
