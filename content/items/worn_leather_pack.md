# Worn Leather Pack

![[assets/items/worn_leather_pack.png|150]]


### Core Properties
- **Rarity**: common
- **Category**: Junk
- **Description**: Decayed travel pack. Straps and buckles yield some materials.

## Usage
- Deconstruct at Base for materials.

## Deconstruction Yields
> **Note**: Yields are probabilistic. Each material is rolled independently; at low **[[skills/salvage|Salvage]]** levels, failing to retrieve any materials is possible.

- **Primary Yields**: [[items/timber|Timber]] (40%), [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] (20%)
- **Rare Yields**: [[items/glowing_mushroom|Glowing Mushroom]] (5%)
