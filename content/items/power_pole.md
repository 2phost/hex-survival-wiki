# Power Pole
**Rarity**: common
**Category**: Infrastructure
**Description**: Deployable relay pole. Needed for the Place Pole action.

## Deconstruction Yields
- **Primary Yields**: [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]] (50%), [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] (40%)

## Usage
- Used to permanently illuminate a hex (0-hex radius).
- Must be connected to the town's power grid to function.
