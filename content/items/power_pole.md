# Power Pole

![[assets/builds/power_pole.png|150]]


### Core Properties
- **Rarity**: common
- **Category**: Power
- **Description**: Deployable relay pole. Needed for the Place Pole action.

## Usage
- Deconstruct at Base for materials.

## Deconstruction Yields
> **Note**: Yields are probabilistic. Each material is rolled independently; at low **[[skills/salvage|Salvage]]** levels, failing to retrieve any materials is possible.

- **Primary Yields**: [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]] (50%), [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] (40%)
