# Power Pole

![[assets/builds/power_pole.png|150]]


### Core Properties
- **Rarity**: common
- **Category**: Power
- **Description**: Deployable relay pole. Needed for the Place Pole action.

## Usage
- Deconstruct at Base for materials.
- Used to place a permanent relay pole on your current hex.

## Obtained From Crafting
This item can be created by combining materials in your inventory.
- **Recipe**: 1 <img src="../assets/items/copper_wiring.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]] + 1 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]]
- **Skill Requirement**: None

## Deconstruction Yields
> **Note**: Yields are probabilistic. Each material is rolled independently; at low **[[skills/salvage|Salvage]]** levels, failing to retrieve any materials is possible.

- **Primary Yields**: <img src="../assets/items/copper_wiring.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]] (50%), <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] (40%)
