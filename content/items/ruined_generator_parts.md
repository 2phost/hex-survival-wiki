# Ruined Generator Parts

![[assets/items/ruined_generator_parts.png|150]]


### Core Properties
- **Rarity**: common
- **Category**: Junk
- **Description**: Scorched generator internals. Mixed scrap and chemicals.

## Usage
- Deconstruct at Base for materials.

## Deconstruction Yields
> **Note**: Yields are probabilistic. Each material is rolled independently; at low **[[skills/salvage|Salvage]]** levels, failing to retrieve any materials is possible.

- **Primary Yields**: [[items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] (50%), [[items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]] (30%), [[items/chemical_sludge|Chemical Sludge]] (20%)
- **Rare Yields**: [[items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]] (6%)
