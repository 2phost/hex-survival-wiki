# Stim Injector
**Rarity**: rare
**Category**: Consumable
**Description**: Refined stimulant injector that restores 4 AP. Any AP over your max is lost. Increases hunger and thirst.

## Usage
- Usage: Consume to add 10 hunger penalty and add 10 thirst penalty and restore 4 AP.

## Consumable Stats
- **Hunger**: 10
- **Thirst**: 10
- **AP**: 4

## Skill Requirements
- [[skills/stim_conditioning|Stim Conditioning]] Level 2
