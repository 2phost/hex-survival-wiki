# Skill: [[skills/scavenger_eye|Scavenger's Eye]]
- **Description**: Heightens your ability to spot resources in the wasteland.
- **Max Level**: 5
- **Effect**: +5% higher success chance per search level.
- **Prerequisites**: [[skills/ap_capacity|AP Capacity]] Lvl 1.

## Unlocks
- **At Level 1**: Enables [[skills/salvage|Salvage]].
- **At Level 2**: Enables [[skills/field_engineering|Field Engineering]].
