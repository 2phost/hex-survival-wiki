# Item: [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]]

![[assets/items/scrap_metal.png|150]]

## Description
Rusted bits of iron and steel salvaged from the pre-collapse ruins. The most fundamental building block for town infrastructure.

## Usage
### 🏗️ Construction
Scrap Metal is required for almost all town facilities and upgrades.
- **[[Base/constructions#Barbed Wire|Barbed Wire Perimeter]]**: 2 Scrap
- **[[Base/constructions#Reinforced Bulkhead|Reinforced Steel Bulkhead]]**: 1 Scrap
- **[[Base/constructions#Scrap Workshop|Scrap Workshop]]**: 1 Scrap
- **[[Base/constructions#Signal Booster|Signal Booster (Radar)]]**: 1 Scrap
- **[[Base/constructions#Hydroponic Patch|Hydroponic Patch]]**: 1 Scrap
- **[[Base/constructions#Automated Sentry|Automated Sentry]]**: 2 Scrap
