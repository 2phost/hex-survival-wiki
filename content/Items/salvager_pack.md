# Item: [[Items/salvager_pack|Salvager Pack]]

![[assets/items/worn_leather_pack.png|150]]

A reinforced scav bag with extra loops and pouches. Increases carried inventory by +2 slots while carried. Rare to find, but can be assembled from field materials.

## Where to Find
- **[[Biomes/hidden_vault|Hidden Vault]]** (0.5%)
- **[[Biomes/electronic_lab|Electronic Store / Lab]]** (0.2%)
- **[[Biomes/industrial|Industrial Zone]]** (0.2%)
- **[[Biomes/ruined_city|Ruined City]]** (0.2%)

## Usage
### Crafting
- Used to craft [[Items/expedition_pack|Expedition Pack]]
