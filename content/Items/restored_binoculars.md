![[assets/items/broken_binoculars.png|150]]
*(Uses broken binoculars icon)*

## Description
Repaired optics that improve field awareness. These binoculars allow a survivor to see the biome of adjacent hexes without having to move onto them.

## 🔦 Effect
- **Reveal Adjacent Biomes**: While moving, adjacent hexes will reveal their biome type (e.g., Forest, Ruined City) instead of remaining hidden.

## 🛠️ Crafting Recipe
Requires an **Optic Repair Kit** combination.
