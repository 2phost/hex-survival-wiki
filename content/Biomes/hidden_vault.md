- Only source of **<img src="../assets/items/ancient_relic.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/ancient_relic|Ancient Relic]]** (Weight 100).

- Primary location for high-tier **Fuel** and **Research**.
