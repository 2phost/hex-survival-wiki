# Biome: [[Biomes/industrial|Industrial Zone]]

<div align="center">

![[assets/tiles/industrial_01.png|250]]

</div>


**Description**: Abandoned factories and heavy machinery centers.

## Strategic Importance
- Primary source of **<img src="../assets/items/chemical_sludge.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/chemical_sludge|Chemical Sludge]]** (Weight 70) and **<img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]]** (Weight 60).
- Best source for **<img src="../assets/items/pressure_valve.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/pressure_valve|Pressure Valve]]** (Weight 42) and **<img src="../assets/items/empty_canister.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/empty_canister|Empty Canister]]** (Weight 45).
- Essential for producing **Alloy Plates** and **Hydraulic Pistons**.

## Facility Fabrication
This facility allows for the production of heavy mechanical parts via the **[[Mechanisms/facility_fabrication|Fabricate]]** action.
- **Action**: Fabricate (**0 AP**)
- **Products**: **<img src="../assets/items/alloy_plate.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/alloy_plate|Alloy Plate]]** (Lvl 1) or **<img src="../assets/items/hydraulic_piston.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/hydraulic_piston|Hydraulic Piston]]** (Lvl 2).
- **Requirement**: **[[Skills/field_engineering|Field Engineering]]** Skill.
- **Power Cost**: Increases town daily power drain by **20%** while active.
