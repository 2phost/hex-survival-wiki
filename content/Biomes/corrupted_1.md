**Description**: A low-intensity zone of corruption that serves as a primary spawning ground for monster hordes.

## Tactical Significance
- **Monster Spawning**: At **[[Mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Midnight]]**, this tile has a chance to spawn a new monster horde.
- **Horde Gathering**: Newly spawned hordes will spend one night **Gathering** on this tile before beginning their march toward the town.
- **Strategic Advice**: Clear these tiles early to prevent hordes from accumulating.

## Loot Tables
Corrupted tiles do not currently yield any scavengeable items and are considered high-risk zones.
