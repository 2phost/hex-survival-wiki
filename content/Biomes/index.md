# Hex Survival Biomes

Explore the different hexagonal tiles and facilities of the world. Each biome has unique resource concentrations and strategic value.

## Biome Directory

### <img src="../assets/tiles/farm_facility_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/farm_facility|Human Farm Facility]]
The primary source of food. High concentration of **Rations** and **Salad**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/hidden_vault_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/hidden_vault|Hidden Vault]]
Rare bunkers containing **Ancient Relics**, **Cargo Drones**, and high-value research materials.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/oasis_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/oasis|Oasis]]
Rare fertile areas and the primary source of **Clean Water**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/electronics_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/electronic_lab|Electronic Store / Lab]]
High-tech facilities containing **Circuit Boards**, **Batteries**, and **Targeting Relays**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/industrial_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/industrial|Industrial Zone]]
Processing complexes and the primary source for **Ballistic Mesh**, **Chemical Sludge**, and **Fuel**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/ruined_city_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/ruined_city|Ruined City]]
Dense urban ruins and the primary source for **Scrap Metal**, **Fortified Rebar**, and salvageable junk.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/forest_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/forest|Forest]]
Wooded areas and the primary source for **Raw Timber** and **Glowing Mushrooms**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/mountain_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/mountain|Mountain / Quarry]]
Rocky terrain and the primary source for **Hardened Stone** and **Quarry Bolts**.

### <img src="../assets/tiles/desert_01.png" width="30" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 8px;"> [[Biomes/desert|Desert / Sand]]
Barren terrain providing **Hardened Stone** and **Raw Timber**.
