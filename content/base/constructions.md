# Base: Constructions

Base constructions are specialized projects that provide town-wide benefits. These are built using the shared resources from the **Town Bank**.

## Utility & Navigation
These structures help the town find resources and explore more efficiently.

| Structure | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Watchtower** | 2 Timber, 2 Stone | Reveals headings of world objectives. |
| **Scrap Workshop** | 1 Scrap, 2 Circuit Boards | -25% Search AP cost within 1 hex of base. |
| **Signal Booster (Radar)** | 1 Scrap, 1 Circuit Boards | Reveals nearest High Value Loot hex every morning. |

## Resource Production
These structures automate the collection of vital resources at **[[mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Midnight]]**.

| Structure | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Well** | 2 Timber, 3 Stone | Produces **10 Water** daily. |
| **Hydroponic Patch** | 2 Timber, 1 Stone, 1 Scrap | Produces rations and fresh salad daily. |
| **Fuel Refinery** | 3 Sludge, 3 Scrap, 2 Stone | Converts salvaged chemicals into stable fuel for town generators. |

## Town Defense
Defensive structures protect the town during the **[[mechanisms/midnight_cycle|Nightly Siege]]**.

| Structure | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Barbed Wire Perimeter** | 2 Scrap Metal | Slows enemy movement by 1 hex; minor chip damage. |
| **Reinforced Steel Bulkhead**| 1 Scrap, 3 Stone | Massive HP boost; small enemies cannot breach for 2 turns. |
| **Automated Sentry** | 2 Scrap, 1 Circuit Boards | Fires at nearest enemy during Nightly Siege. Ammo: 10/night. |
