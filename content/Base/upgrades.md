# Base: Advanced Upgrades

Advanced upgrades represent significant long-term investments that increase the town's sustainability and technological capabilities.

## Power & Sustainability
Reducing drain and increasing capacity is critical for surviving the late game.

| Upgrade | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solar Panels** | 2 <img src="../assets/items/solar_cell.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/solar_cell|Solar Cell]], 1 <img src="../assets/items/circuit_board.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]], 2 <img src="../assets/items/copper_wiring.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]], 1 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] | Reduces daily base power drain by **10%**. |
| **Battery Bank** | 3 <img src="../assets/items/car_battery.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/car_battery|Car Battery]], 4 <img src="../assets/items/copper_wiring.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]], 2 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] | Increases maximum town power capacity by **+20%** permanently. |
| **Beacon Amplifier** | 3 <img src="../assets/items/circuit_board.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]], 3 <img src="../assets/items/copper_wiring.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/copper_wiring|Copper Wiring]], 2 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] | Beacon control systems that improve movement efficiency in darkness. |

## Research & Technology
The Research Lab unlocks the town's ability to process advanced data and materials.

| Upgrade | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Research Lab** | 4 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] | Enables research projects and data processing. |
| **Research Lab II** | 60 <img src="../assets/items/scrap_metal.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/scrap_metal|Scrap Metal]] | +20% research speed, +1 research slot. |

## Build Pathing
- **Research Priority**: Constructing the **Research Lab** early allows the town to begin processing **<img src="../assets/items/research_material.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[Items/research_material|Research Material]]** found in Labs and Vaults.
- **Sustainability**: As the town's power grid expands via **[[Items/power_pole|Power Poles]]**, building **Solar Panels** becomes mandatory to offset the increased passive drain.
