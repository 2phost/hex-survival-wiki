# Mechanism: [[Mechanisms/inventory_management|Inventory Management]]

## Capacity
- **Default Slots**: Players start with a limited carrying capacity of **5 slots**.
- **Expansion**: 
 - The "Pack Mule" skill can increase this by up to +3 slots (total of 8).
 - Capacity can be further expanded by rare craftable **Backpack Tiers**:
 - **[[Items/salvager_pack|Salvager Pack]]**: +2 slots
 - **[[Items/expedition_pack|Expedition Pack]]**: +3 slots
 - **[[Items/hauler_pack|Hauler Pack]]**: +4 slots
- **Dropped Packs**: Carrying capacity shrinks if a backpack is dropped; any overflow items are automatically dropped on the ground.

## Cargo Drone Logistics
Players can utilize **Cargo Drones** to manage resources more efficiently while in the field.
- **Function**: Load up to 4 items into a Cargo Drone and send them back to the **Town Bank** directly from the field.
- **Requirement**: Requires **[[Skills/drone_operator|Drone Operator Level 2]]**.

## [[Items/index|Items]] on the Ground
- **Discovery**: When scavenging or in specific locations, items can appear "on the ground."
- **Interaction**: Players can see and interact with these items without they being in their active inventory slots.
- **Selection**: Limited slots force players to prioritize which resources to carry and which to leave behind or use immediately.
