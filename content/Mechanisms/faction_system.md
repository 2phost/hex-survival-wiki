# Mechanism: [[Mechanisms/faction_system|Town Faction System]]

The Town Faction system, introduced on March 20, 2026, represents the town's standing with Headquarters. High faction levels lead to better support and resources.

## Faction Gain
- **[[Mechanisms/expedition_objectives|Command Objectives]]**: Completing main or side tasks grants Faction points for the entire town.
- **Sustained Performance**: Consistently completing objectives builds support over time, allowing the town to progress through higher support tiers.

## Headquarters (HQ) Supply Reports
HQ monitors town progress and delivers supplies based on performance grades.
- **Timing**: Supply reports are issued at **Midnight** on **Days 5, 10, 15, and 20**.
- **Grading**: Each report grades the town's task completion for the previous 5-day period.
- **Rewards**: Scaling rewards are delivered directly into town storage:
    - **Poor Performance**: Common emergency supplies.
    - **Perfect Execution**: Major Rare or Mythic shipments.

## Morning Briefings
The daily Morning Briefing now includes a breakdown of the faction system:
- **Faction Gained**: Shows the points earned in the previous 24 hours.
- **Total Faction**: Displays the current cumulative standing.
- **Shipment Details**: Explains the contents and reason for any HQ resupply packages received.
