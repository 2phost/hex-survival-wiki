**Description**: A rare, fortified pre-collapse bunker.

## Strategic Importance
- The only source for **[[items/ancient_relic|Ancient Relics]]** (Weight 100).
- High concentration of tech: **<img src="../assets/items/glowing_mushroom.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/glowing_mushroom|Glowing Mushrooms]]** (Weight 45), **<img src="../assets/items/research_material.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/research_material|Research Material]]** (Weight 35), and **<img src="../assets/items/circuit_board.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]]** (Weight 25).
- Excellent source for **<img src="../assets/items/gasoline_generator.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/gasoline_generator_empty|Gasoline Generator (empty)]]** (Weight 40) and **<img src="../assets/items/gasoline_canister.png" width="20" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 4px;"> [[items/gasoline_canister|Gasoline Canister]]** (Weight 20).
