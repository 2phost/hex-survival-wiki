# Biome: [[biomes/hidden_vault|Hidden Vault]]

![[assets/tiles/hidden_vault_01.png|200]]


**Description**: A rare, fortified pre-collapse bunker.

## Strategic Importance
- The only source for **[[items/ancient_relic|Ancient Relics]]** (Weight 100).
- High concentration of tech: **[[items/glowing_mushroom|Glowing Mushrooms]]** (Weight 45), **[[items/research_material|Research Material]]** (Weight 35), and **[[items/circuit_boards|Circuit Boards]]** (Weight 25).
- Excellent source for **[[items/gasoline_generator_empty|Gasoline Generator (empty)]]** (Weight 40) and **[[items/gasoline_canister|Gasoline Canister]]** (Weight 20).
