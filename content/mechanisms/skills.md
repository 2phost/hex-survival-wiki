# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]]

## Overview
[[mechanisms/skills|Skills]] are permanent character upgrades that persist between "towns." They represent long-term progression and specialize the player's capabilities.

## Progression Rules
- **Real-Time Learning**: [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]] take actual time to learn (e.g., ~24 hours for some levels).
- **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Cost**: Starting, stopping, or pausing a skill consumes **0 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]**.
- **Concurrency & Pausing**: Only one skill can be actively researched at a time, but players can **pause** one skill to start another without losing progress.

## Available [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]]

### Action & Energy
- **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity**: +1 max [[mechanisms/action_points|Action Points]] per level. 
    - **Lvl 1**: 1 hour of real time.
    - **Lvl 2**: 24 hours of real time.
- **Rest Recovery**: Grants a chance for +1 extra [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] each rest period (per hour): 10% per level (up to 50%).
- **Stim Conditioning**: Trains metabolism to use [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] recovery stims. 
    - **Lvl 1**: 1 hour of real time. Required to use basic Stim Packs.
    - **Lvl 2**: 24 hours of real time. Unlocks use of rare stims.
    - **Lvl 3**: Unlocks mythic stims.

### [[mechanisms/exploration|Exploration]] & Inventory
- **Scavenger's Eye**: +5% loot chance when searching per level. Improves the "roll" vs the encounter rather than auto-finding.
- **Pack Mule**: +1 carried inventory slot per level (max +3).

### Engineering & [[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]]
- **Field Engineering**: Required to operate and calibrate advanced facilities (Industrial and Electronic Lab tiles).
- **Generator Operations**: Required to run and refuel the town generator. Higher levels unlock advanced fuel types.

### Resource Management
- **Salvage**: Unlocks and improves item deconstruction into base resources.
    - **Level 1**: Unlocks deconstruction.
    - **Higher Levels**: +10% base yield and +5% chance for rare bonus materials per level.
