# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]]

## Overview
[[mechanisms/skills|Skills]] are permanent character upgrades that persist between "towns." They represent long-term progression and specialize the player's capabilities.

## Progression Rules
- **Real-Time Learning**: [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]] take actual time to learn.
- **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Cost**: Starting, stopping, or pausing a skill consumes **0 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]**.
- **Concurrency & Pausing**: Only one skill can be actively researched at a time, but players can **pause** one skill to start another without losing progress.

## Standard Training Times
Most skills follow this progression (all confirmed):
- **Lvl 1**: 1 hour.
- **Lvl 2**: 24 hours (1 day).
- **Lvl 3**: 168 hours (7 days).
- **Lvl 4**: 336 hours (14 days).
- **Lvl 5**: 720 hours (30 days).

## Skill Categories

### ⚡ Action & Energy
#### **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity**
- **Effect**: +1 max [[mechanisms/action_points|Action Points]] per level.
- **Prerequisites**: None.
- **Max Level**: 5.

#### **Rest Recovery**
- **Effect**: Grants a 10% chance per level (up to 50%) for +1 extra [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] during each hourly rest period.
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity Lvl 2** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 5.

#### **Stim Conditioning**
- **Effect**: Trains metabolism to use [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] recovery stims.
- **Levels**: Unlocks Stim Packs (Lvl 1), Stim Injectors (Lvl 2), and Stim Overdrive (Lvl 3).
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity Lvl 1** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 3.

### 🔍 [[mechanisms/exploration|Exploration]] & Inventory
#### **Scavenger's Eye**
- **Effect**: +5% higher chance to find loot per search roll.
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity Lvl 1** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 5.

#### **Pack Mule**
- **Effect**: +1 carried inventory slot per level (max +3).
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Capacity Lvl 1** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 3.

### ⚙️ Engineering & [[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]]
#### **Field Engineering**
- **Effect**: Required to operate Industrial and Electronic Lab facilities (production).
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/exploration|Scavenger's Eye]] Lvl 2** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 3.

#### **Generator Operations**
- **Effect**: Required to refuel town generators. Higher levels unlock higher-tier fuels.
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/skills|Field Engineering]] Lvl 1** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 3.

### ♻️ Resource Management
#### **Salvage**
- **Effect**: Unlocks and improves item deconstruction into base resources.
- **Bonus**: +10% base yield and +5% rare chance per level.
- **Prerequisite**: Requires **[[mechanisms/exploration|Scavenger's Eye]] Lvl 1** to unlock.
- **Max Level**: 5.
