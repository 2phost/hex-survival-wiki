# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]]

## Overview
[[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]] consists of permanent structures players build and place onto hexagonal tiles to modify the environment or provide town-wide benefits.

## Power Poles
The most fundamental infrastructure for safe exploration.
- **Recipe**: 1 Copper Wiring + 1 Scrap Metal.
- **Placement Rule**: Must be placed in a tile adjacent (next) to another tile that already contains a Power Pole or the Base.
- **Function**: Extends the town's power grid to the new tile, providing permanent **Light** to that specific hex.
- **Range**: A Power Pole **only lights its own tile**; it does not provide light to adjacent hexes.
- **Strategic Impact**: Allows players to create safe "highways" or "grids" across the map, eliminating the risk of being "Consumed by Darkness" in those hexes.

## Grid Connectivity
Power Poles serve as the physical link between the town's central energy source and specialized facilities.
- **Status Indicator**: When a tile containing a building is successfully connected to the grid, it displays: **"Grid Power: Online ready"**.
- **Operational Requirement**: Specialized tiles (e.g., [[Industrial Zone]], Electronic Lab) cannot function or start production unless they are "Online."
- **Visual Feedback**: This provides clear confirmation to the player that their "Power Highway" has successfully reached its destination.

## Base Constructions
Unlike Power Poles, which are placed on individual hexes, these projects are managed through the **"Enter Base"** menu and provide town-wide benefits or resource generation.

- **[[Watchtower]]**: Reveals objective directions.
- **[[Hydroponic Patch]]**: Daily food production.
- **[[Well]]**: Daily water production (10 units).
- **[[Solar Panels]]**: Power drain reduction (-10%).
- **[[Research Lab]]**: Unlocks research projects.
- **[[Battery Storage]]**: Increases max power capacity (+20%).

For full details on costs and effects, see **[[mechanisms/base_and_resources|Base and Resources]]**.
