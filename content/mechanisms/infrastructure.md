# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]] and Base Construction

## Base Builds
Players can contribute resources to the Base to construct defensive and utility structures.

| Structure | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Barbed Wire** | 2 Scrap Metal | Slows enemies by 1 hex; minor damage. |
| **Steel Bulkhead** | 1 Scrap, 3 Stone | Massive HP boost; blocks small enemies for 2 turns. |
| **Watchtower** | 2 Timber, 2 Stone | Reveals direction of active world threats. |
| **Scrap Workshop** | 1 Scrap, 2 Circuit Boards | -25% AP cost for searching within 1 hex of base. |
| **Signal Booster** | 1 Scrap, 1 Circuit Boards | Reveals nearest High Value Loot hex every morning. |
| **Hydroponic Patch** | 2 Timber, 1 Stone, 1 Scrap | Produces rations and fresh salad daily. |
| **Well** | 2 Timber, 3 Stone | Produces 10 water daily at midnight. |
| **Automated Sentry** | 2 Scrap, 1 Circuit Boards | Defensive fire during Nightly Siege (10 ammo/night). |

| **Research Lab II** | 60 Scrap Metal | +1 Research Slot; +20% Research Speed (Req: Lab I). |

## Power Grid
- **Deployment**: **Power Poles** must be placed on the player's current tile using the **Place Pole** action.
- **Connectivity**: Poles must be connected to the town's power chain to function. A new pole can only be placed on a tile adjacent to an existing, powered pole or the Base.
- **Light Range**: A powered pole illuminates only the **current tile (0-hex radius)**. It provides safety from the darkness and allows for safe movement and scavenging on that tile.
- **Industrial Support**: Powered poles adjacent to industrial tiles (Industrial Zone, Electronic Lab) allow those tiles to be powered for production, provided the **[[mechanisms/skills|Field Engineering]]** skill requirement is met.

## Maintenance
Infrastructure may require "Power" from the city's central grid to remain active. Solar Panels are critical for long-term sustainability by reducing the passive drain on the Generators.
