# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/expedition_objectives|Expedition Objectives]]

## Overview
[[mechanisms/expedition_objectives|Expedition Objectives]] are time-sensitive tasks that provide rewards upon completion or penalties if ignored.

## Current Expedition: [[Tile: Relay|The Corrupted Relay]]
- **Status**: **Heading Hidden**.
- **Active Task**: **[[Watchtower|Build Watchtower]]**.
- **Dependency**: The Relay's location (heading) is currently unknown. The town must first construct a **[[Watchtower]]** in the Base to reveal the direction of the objective.

## Objective Types

### 1. Story & Infrastructure Events (e.g., Build Watchtower)
- **Goal**: Construct a specific building in the Base to progress the story or unlock navigation data.
- **Reward**: Reveals the headings of hidden world objectives (like Relays or Scavenge sites).
- **Meta-Strategy**: Building the Watchtower is the first priority for any town that wants to expand beyond blind exploration.

### 2. Threat Mitigation (e.g., Corrupted Relay)
- **Difficulty**: High.
- **Goal**: Neutralize a specific threat on the map.
- **Penalty if Ignored**: Global negative effects, such as reduced search chances town-wide, which worsen over time (e.g., -8% initially, then -12% at midnight).
- **Requirements**:
    - **Navigation**: **Requires [[Watchtower]] to reveal headings.**
    - **Proximity**: Reach a specific hex within a certain distance.
    - **Action**: Spend [[mechanisms/action_points|Action Points]] ([[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]) to perform a specific task (e.g., "Disable" for 2 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]).
