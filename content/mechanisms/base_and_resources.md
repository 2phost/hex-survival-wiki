# Mechanism: Base and Resources

## The [[Tile: Base|Base]] Tile
The Base is the heart of the town. It provides inherent light and serves as the primary hub for resource storage and infrastructure development.

### "Enter Base" Menu
When a player is standing on the **[[Tile: Base]]** hex, a new **"Enter Base"** button appears on the main bottom menu. Clicking this opens a specialized management interface.

## 1. Town Status & Power
The top of the Base menu displays the town's current survival status.
- **Light & Survival Bar**: A percentage meter (e.g., **72%**) indicating the town's current power levels. 
- **Survival Condition**: **At 0%, the town falls.** This is the primary game-over condition.
- **Night Use**: Displays the predicted power consumption for the upcoming midnight transition (e.g., **"Night Use: 20%"**).

## 2. Town Resources (The Bank)
The Base contains a shared storage system known as the **Town Resources** section.
- **Shared Inventory**: Shows all resources currently stored in the base and their quantities.
- **Depositing**: Clicking items in the player's personal inventory while in the Base menu moves them to the **Bank**.
- **Withdrawing**: Players can pick items from the Bank to add them to their personal inventory.
- **Confirmed Resources**: 
    - **Vitals**: Rations, Fresh Drinking Water, Fresh Salad.
    - **Materials**: Timber, Stone, Scrap Metal, Copper Wiring.
    - **Tech**: Batteries, Car Battery, Solar Cell, Circuit Boards.

## 3. Base Constructions
The Base menu lists advanced projects that can be built using shared resources from the Bank.

| Construction | Requirements | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **[[Watchtower]]** | 2 Timber, 2 Stone | Simple lookout tower that reveals the direction of the active world objective. **Currently required to reveal the Relay.** |
| **[[Hydroponic Patch]]** | 2 Timber, 1 Stone, 1 Scrap Metal | Produces **Rations** and **Fresh Salad** at the base each day. |
| **[[Well]]** | 2 Timber, 3 Stone | Produces **10 Water** at the base each day at [[Midnight]]. |
| **[[Solar Panels]]** | 2 Solar Cell, 1 Circuit Boards, 2 Copper Wiring, 1 Scrap Metal | Reduces daily base power drain by **10%**. |
| **[[Research Lab]]** | 4 Scrap Metal | Enables research projects and lets the town process Research data. |
| **[[Battery Storage]]** | 3 Car Battery, 4 Copper Wiring, 2 Scrap Metal | Increases maximum town power capacity by **+20%** permanently. |
