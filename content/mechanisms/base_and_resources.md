# Mechanism: Base and Resources

## Base Stores
The base serves as a central hub for resource management. Resources are consumed or added at specific intervals (e.g., [[Midnight]]).

### Key Resources
- **Rations**: Food supply for survival.
- **Water**: Essential hydration.
- **Batteries**: Technical resource used for objectives or equipment.

## Buildings
Constructing buildings unlocks new capabilities or reveals information.
- **Watchtower**: Used to reveal headings for distant objectives or points of interest.
