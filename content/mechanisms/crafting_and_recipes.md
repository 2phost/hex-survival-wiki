# Mechanism: Crafting and Recipes

## Overview
Crafting allows players to combine base resources or damaged items into functional equipment and infrastructure.

## Crafting Logic
- **Input/Output**: Recipes require specific quantities of input items to produce a single output item.
- **Recipe Unlocking**: Many recipes start as "Locked," likely requiring specific items in the inventory or character levels/skills to reveal.
- **The "Combine" Action**: A manual trigger in the Inventory to execute the recipe.

## Known Recipes

| Output | Inputs | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Functioning Lamp** | 1 Battery + 1 Lamp (empty) | Available |
| **Glowing Bottle** | 1 Old Glass Bottle + 1 Glowing Mushroom | Locked |
| **Gasoline Generator** | 1 Gasoline Generator (empty) + 1 Gasoline Canister | Locked |
| **Solar Cell** | 1 Circuit Boards + 2 Copper Wiring + 1 Scrap Metal | Locked |
| **Power Pole** | 1 Copper Wiring + 1 Scrap Metal | Available |

## Strategic Importance
Crafting moves players from simple survival (finding food/water) to infrastructure development (power poles, solar cells), which likely ties into the town-wide Power system.
