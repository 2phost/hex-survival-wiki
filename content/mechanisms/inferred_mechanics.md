# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/inferred_mechanics|Inferred Mechanics]] & Logic

These are logical deductions based on established gameplay patterns and official help text. They serve as "working theories" until confirmed by direct observation.

## 1. The Resource Economy Circuit
Based on the specialized tiles and crafting recipes, the game likely follows a "Hub and Spoke" resource model:
- **[[Industrial Zone]] / Quarry**: Supplies the "Scrap Metal" and "Fuel" needed for basic infrastructure ([[Power Poles]], Generators).
- **[[Electronic Store]] / Lab**: Supplies the "Wiring" and "Circuit Boards" needed for advanced tech (Solar Cells, High-tier Stims).
- **[[Oasis]] / Human Farm / Forest**: Supplies the "Water," "Rations," and "Biologicals" needed for survival vitals.
- **Inference**: High-level play involves building "Power Highways" specifically to connect these hubs back to the Base.

## 2. The [[Midnight]] Cycle (The "Toll")
- **Confirmed**: Rations and Water are added to base stores at [[Midnight]].
- **Inferred**: Survival vitals ([[Hunger]]/[[Thirst]]) and Town Power Drain are likely deducted at [[Midnight]]. This creates a daily "survival check" where the town must have enough resources in the bank to survive the transition to the next day.

## 3. Escalating Threats & Defense
- **The Help Menu mentions "Defenses"**: This suggests that simply lighting a tile might not be enough. There may be "Darkness Events" or "Raids" that attempt to destroy [[Power Poles]] or drain the Base.
- **Inference**: Players may eventually need to craft "Defense Turrets" or "Reinforced Poles" to protect the grid from escalating threats.

## 4. [[mechanisms/skills|Skills]] as Profession Gates
- **Inference**: The skills system is designed to force player specialization (Cooperation). 
    - One player might focus on **Field Engineering** (The Builder).
    - Another on **Scavenger's Eye / Pack Mule** (The Gatherer).
    - Another on **Generator Ops / Salvage** (The Technician).
- This reinforces the collaborative nature: a town needs all types of specialists to survive long-term.

## 5. Narrative Inferences (The Setting)
- **"Corrupted Relay" & "Human Farm"**: Suggest a world where humanity is either a resource being harvested or a remnant of a failed technological utopia. 
- **[[mechanisms/light_and_darkness|The Darkness]]**: May be a "Nanite Cloud," a "Corrupted Network," or a "Biological Plague" rather than just a lack of photons.
