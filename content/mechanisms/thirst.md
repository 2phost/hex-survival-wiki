# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/thirst|Thirst]]

Thirst is a primary survival vital in Hex Survival. Because it drains daily at **[[Midnight]]**, dehydration is a constant threat for any town without a reliable water source.

## 1. The [[Midnight]] Drain
At **[[Midnight]]**, every player's thirst bar is reduced by **40% (4 bars out of 10)**. This "Midnight Toll" is mandatory for survival and must be addressed before the transition to a new day.

## 2. Restoring Thirst
Thirst is primarily restored by consuming **Clean Water**. Note that high-level stims will *increase* thirst as a side effect.

| Item | Thirst Restoration | Requirements |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Clean Water** | **+30** | None |
| **Stim Pack** | **-6** | Stim Cond. Lvl 1 |
| **Stim Injector** | **-10**| Stim Cond. Lvl 2 |
| **Stim Overdrive** | **-14**| Stim Cond. Lvl 3 |

## 3. Where to Find Water

### Base Infrastructure
Building specialized facilities at the **[[Tile: Base|Base]]** provides a reliable, daily source of hydration for the town.
- **[[Well]]**: Produces **10 Water** at [[Midnight]]. Resources are added to the Town Bank.

### Scavenging Locations
If the base production is insufficient, players must scavenge the wasteland.
- **[[Oasis]]**: The primary source of **Clean Water** (Weight 90).
- **[[Forest]]**: Good source of **Clean Water** (Weight 40).
- **[[Farm Facility]]**: Moderate source of **Clean Water** (Weight 35).
- **[[Ruined City]]**: Small amounts of **Clean Water** can be found here.
