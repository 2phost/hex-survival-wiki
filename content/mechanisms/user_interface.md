# Mechanism: [[mechanisms/user_interface|User Interface]] and Navigation

## Main Navigation (Bottom Bar)
The primary interface for player actions is located at the bottom of the screen and includes:
- **Scavenge**: Accesses the map and current hex interaction tools.
- **Inventory**: Manages carried items, equipment, resources, and access to the **Recipes** menu.
- **[[mechanisms/skills|Skills]]**: Opens the permanent progression menu for character upgrades.
- **Chat**: Provides communication with other survivors. Opens in the **bottom right** of the screen.

## Dynamic Action System
The UI is context-sensitive. Specific buttons and actions unlock only when certain conditions are met:
- **Location-Based**: 
    - **[[Tile: Base]]**: Unlocks the **"Enter Base"** button on the bottom menu, granting access to the Town Bank and Construction projects.
    - **Relay**: Likely unlocks unique interaction buttons for signal transmission.
- **Requirement-Based**: Having specific items or skills (e.g., "Field Engineering") may reveal previously hidden options.
- **Event-Based**: Current [[mechanisms/expedition_objectives|Expedition Objectives]] may trigger temporary buttons for quest-specific actions.

## Notes
- The "Chat" button reinforces the collaborative nature of the game, allowing for coordination on objectives.
- Context-sensitive buttons help keep the interface clean while guiding players toward relevant tasks.
