# Skill: [[Skills/power_infrastructure|Power Infrastructure]]

Install and recover field relay poles. Level 1 allows placing power poles. Level 2 allows dismantling existing poles with a chance to recover the item. Level 3 unlocks placement of Reinforced Power Poles.

- **Max Level**: 3

## Training Time
- **Level 1**: 1 hour
- **Level 2**: 1 day
- **Level 3**: 7 days

## Prerequisites
- [[Skills/field_engineering|Field Engineering]] Level 1

## Effects
- Unlocks placing and dismantling power poles, and Reinforced Power Poles at higher levels.
