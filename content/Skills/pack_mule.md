# Skill: [[Skills/pack_mule|Pack Mule]]

+1 carried inventory slot per level (max +3). 1 item always = 1 slot.

- **Max Level**: 3

## Training Time
- **Level 1**: 1 hour
- **Level 2**: 1 day
- **Level 3**: 7 days

## Prerequisites
- [[Skills/ap_capacity|AP Capacity]] Level 1

## Effects
- +1 carried inventory slot per level.
