# Skill: [[Skills/weapon_maintenance|Weapon Maintenance]]

Weapon Maintenance is a fundamental combat skill for survivors who choose to defend the town. It focuses on the care and preservation of improvised weaponry during combat. See **[[Mechanisms/combat_and_defense|Combat & Defense]]** for rule details.

## 📝 Description
"Reduce weapon break chance when attacking monsters by **2% per level** (up to 10%)."

## 📈 Progression
This skill provides a passive reduction to the break chance of all items used as weapons.

| Level | Break Chance Reduction | Training Time (Real-Time) |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Lvl 1** | **-2%** | **1 Hour** |
| **Lvl 2** | **-4%** | **24 Hours** |
| **Lvl 3** | **-6%** | **7 Days** |
| **Lvl 4** | **-8%** | **14 Days** |
| **Lvl 5** | **-10%** | **30 Days** |

## 🔓 Prerequisites
- **[[Skills/ap_capacity|AP Capacity]]**: Level 1

## ⚔️ Compatible Weapons
- **[[Items/alloy_plate|Alloy Plate]]** (Base: 12% break chance)
- **[[Items/burnt_motor|Burnt-Out Motor]]** (Base: 22% break chance)
- **[[Items/rusty_tool|Rusty Tool]]** (Base: 35% break chance)
