# Skill: [[Skills/rest_recovery|Rest Recovery]]
- **Description**: Gain a chance for +1 extra AP each rest period (per hour): 10% per level, up to 50%.
- **Max Level**: 5
- **Effect**: 10% chance per level (up to 50%) to gain +1 extra AP during the hourly rest cycle.
- **Prerequisites**: [[Skills/ap_capacity|Ap Capacity]] Level 2

## Unlocks
- *None (End of specialization tree)*
