# Skill: [[Skills/stim_conditioning|Stim Conditioning]]
- **Description**: Metabolic training for using AP-restoration stimulants.
- **Max Level**: 3
- **Effect**: Unlocks use of higher-tier stimulants.
  - **Lvl 1**: [[Items/stim_pack|Stim Pack]] (+2 AP).
  - **Lvl 2**: [[Items/stim_injector|Stim Injector]] (+4 AP).
  - **Lvl 3**: [[Items/stim_overdrive|Stim Overdrive]] (+6 AP).
- **Prerequisites**: [[Skills/ap_capacity|AP Capacity]] Lvl 1.
