# Skill: [[Skills/eidetic_memory|Eidetic Memory]]

Remember the lay of the land. Dark (unlit) tiles are hidden by default. Each level lets you see previously explored tiles faintly, in an expanding ring around you. At level 5 you see the full explored map as with the current view.

- **Max Level**: 5

## Training Time
- **Level 1**: 1 hour
- **Level 2**: 1 day
- **Level 3**: 7 days
- **Level 4**: 14 days
- **Level 5**: 30 days

## Prerequisites
- [[Skills/ap_capacity|AP Capacity]] Level 1

## Effects
- Range of remembered tiles increases per level. At level 5, all explored tiles are remembered.
