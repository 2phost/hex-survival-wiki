# Skill: [[Skills/salvage|Salvage]]
- **Description**: Learn to deconstruct items into base resources. Level 1 unlocks deconstruction. Higher levels improve base yield (+10% per level) and increase chance for rare bonus materials (+5% per level).
- **Max Level**: 5
- **Effect**: Unlocks deconstruction at Lvl 1.
 - **Base Yield**: +10% material return per level.
 - **Rare Chance**: +5% rare bonus material chance per level.
- **Prerequisites**: [[Skills/scavenger_eye|Scavenger Eye]] Level 1

## Unlocks
- *None (End of specialization tree)*
