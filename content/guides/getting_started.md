# Guide: [[guides/getting_started|Getting Started]] in Hex Survival

Welcome to the town, survivor. You've joined a collaborative effort to keep the lights on and the darkness at bay. Here’s what you need to know for your first few days.

## 1. The Golden Rule: Stay in the Light
The "Darkness" in this world is lethal.
- **Never move into a dark hex** unless you have a light source (like your **[[Starter Lamp]]**) or you are certain it is within the town's power grid.
- Moving into a dark hex without light results in immediate death: **"Consumed by darkness."**
- **CRITICAL WARNING**: If your lamp shows **"1 day remaining,"** you must be in a permanently lit hex (Base or Power Pole) *before* the [[Midnight]] transition. If your lamp expires while you are in an unlit hex at Day Change, you will die instantly.

## 2. Managing Your Vitals
You have four status bars: **[[Hunger]], [[Thirst]], [[mechanisms/action_points|Action Points]] ([[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]), and Activity.**
- **Find Water and Rations**: Head to an **[[Oasis]]** or the **Human Farm Facility** early on.
- **Consuming [[mechanisms/items|Items]]**: Use **Right-Click** in your inventory to drink water or eat rations.
- **[[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] Management**: Every action (moving, scavenging, questing) costs [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]. 
- **Recovery**: If you run out, set your activity to **Resting** to recover **1 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] per hour**. You can also use a **[[Stim Pack]]** for an immediate +2 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] boost (requires Stim Conditioning Lvl 1).

## 3. Your First Expedition
Check the **[[mechanisms/expedition_objectives|Expedition Objectives]]** (top of the screen).
- **Emergency Stockpile**: Look for **Batteries** in the **Ruined City** or **[[Electronic Store]]/Lab** and deposit them at the **Base**. This helps the whole town get rations and water at midnight.
- **Corrupted Relays**: If these aren't disabled (requires 2 [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]]), search chances town-wide will drop significantly.

## 4. [[mechanisms/scavenging|Scavenging]] and Inventory
- **Hex [[mechanisms/scavenging|Scavenging]]**: Click on your current hex to start scavenging. You can toggle it on/off as needed.
- **Limited Space**: You only have **5 slots**. Prioritize high-value items like Batteries, Copper Wiring, and Scrap Metal.
- **[[mechanisms/items|Items]] on the Ground**: You can leave things on the floor and they will stay there, but beware the **[[bugs/item_overlay|Item Overlay]] Bug** in crowded areas.

## 5. Building [[mechanisms/infrastructure|Infrastructure]]
Once you have **1 Copper Wiring** and **1 Scrap Metal**, use the **Inventory -> Recipes** menu to craft a **Power Pole**.
- **Placement**: Place it on a tile adjacent to the Base or an existing pole.
- **Why?**: This permanently lights up that tile, making it safe for everyone to travel through without a lamp.

## 7. Scouting the Unknown (Fog of War)
The map starts empty. You won't know where the **[[Oasis]]** or **Electronic Lab** is until someone finds it.
- **Scout Early**: Use your **[[Starter Lamp]]** to reveal nearby tiles and find critical resources.
- **Don't Overextend**: Always save enough [[mechanisms/action_points|AP]] to return to the Base or a lit tile before your lamp runs out or you run out of energy.
- **Share Discoveries**: Use the Town Chat to tell others the direction of key buildings (e.g., "[[Industrial Zone]] 4 hexes North").
